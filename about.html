About Intellix

The story of how a small idea in 2025 became the foundation of the next generation of intelligent payments.

Our Origins

Intellix was founded in November 2025 with a bold mission: to redefine how the world moves money. What began as a concept to make payments faster and smarter quickly grew into a vision for a global intelligent payment ecosystem.

Intellix focuses on simplicity, automation, and intelligence.

Looking Forward: The 2026 Roadmap

2026 marks the first major expansion phase of Intellix. Our roadmap is carefully structured to build a powerful, scalable, and globally connected payment platform.

Q1 2026 — Core Platform Launch
Release of our real-time payment engine, fraud protection AI, and onboarding systems.
Q2 2026 — Global Currency Expansion
Integration of 80+ currencies, cross-border smart routing, and instant FX support.
Q3 2026 — Merchant & Business Tools
Subscriptions, invoicing, payout automation, and financial analytics suite.
Q4 2026 — Future Intelligence
AI-assisted financial automation, predictive revenue modeling, and futuristic payment tech.

Our Vision

Intellix aims to become the world’s leading intelligent payment backbone— powering apps, businesses, banks, and next-gen financial ecosystems. We believe the future of payments is smart, automated, global, and effortless.
