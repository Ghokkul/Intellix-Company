Hi. I'm Ghokkul.

I built Intellix in November 2025 because I wanted a place that was completely mine. Not a social media profile. Not a platform somebody else owns. My own corner of the internet.

"I wanted one place where I could write anything that comes to my mind — about the topics I enjoy, the things I build, and the ideas I find worth sharing."

Why I built this

I love technology — how it works, where it's going, and what it means for how we live and work. I love business — the strategy behind building things, the thinking behind decisions. I love design — not just how things look, but how they feel to use.

I didn't want to pick one. So I built a space where I don't have to.

The digital store

I also sell my own digital products here — through my Gumroad store at ghokkuldesigns.gumroad.com. Everything in the store is made by me, from scratch. Design assets, templates, and tools I've built and genuinely use.

Purely mine

Every word on this site is mine. Every product in the store is mine. Every design decision is mine. Intellix is purely my creation and purely my own work. I built it to last — not for an algorithm, not for a trend, but for myself and for anyone who finds something useful here.

If something I write helps you think differently about a problem, that's enough for me.
