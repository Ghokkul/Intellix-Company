⏱ TIMELINE
November 2025 — Founded
The beginning of intelligent payments
Ghokkul Muhunthan founded Intellix with a clear vision: build the payment infrastructure that the world actually deserves. Fast, AI-powered, and social at its core.
December 2025 — FraudNet v1.0
AI Fraud Shield launched
The first version of FraudNet went live — processing risk scores in under 1ms with an explainable AI model that shows exactly why a transaction was flagged, achieving 99.8% accuracy from day one.
January 2026 — Social Pay
Payments become conversations
Intellix Social launched — the first payment platform where you can chat, send money, and request payments all inside the same message thread. No app switching. No friction.
February 2026 — Multi-language SDK
Built in six languages
The Intellix platform expanded to six programming languages — Python, TypeScript, Go, Rust, SQL, and Swift — making it the most developer-friendly payment infrastructure ever built.
March 2026 — Alpha Live
Open to the world
Intellix opened its alpha to developers and businesses worldwide. 135+ currencies, 150+ countries, and a developer API that works in every language your team uses.
◆ WHAT WE STAND FOR
Three pillars. One platform.

Everything we build at Intellix is grounded in a clear set of beliefs about what payments should be.

🎯
Our Mission

Make every payment intelligent — instant routing, real-time fraud detection, and zero friction for every transaction on earth. Payments should work for people, not the other way around.

💡
Our Vision

A world where money moves as freely as information — where sending $500 to a friend feels as natural as sending a message. Financial infrastructure that's invisible in the best possible way.

♥
Our Values

Transparency, speed, and trust. Every product we build is designed to empower users — never to extract from them. We publish our fees, explain our AI decisions, and never sell your data.
