▸ How FraudNet works
AI that explains itself.

Most fraud systems are black boxes — they tell you a transaction was declined, but not why. FraudNet is different. Our hybrid approach combines an Isolation Forest ML model with 8 categories of explainable rule-based signals, so every risk score comes with a full breakdown of what triggered it.
